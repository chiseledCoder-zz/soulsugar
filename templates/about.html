About Us

Welcome!!

Soul Sugar is a business born out of a serious sweet tooth, passion and knowing how food is a central part of celebrations and brings people together. We are fuelled by the desire to make people's occasions all the more special with our treats. Soul Sugar is passion backed by sound professional knowledge . “Soul Sugar” was inspired by the Greek word “Meraki” which means “the soul, creativity, or love put into something; the essence of yourself that is put into your work”. These words highly resonate with all that we do.

Every cake or dessert we make is made from the finest ingredients available, using the best techniques and is baked fresh to order in small batches. It is then hand-decorated by us cake obsessed people to make sure you get the best cake experience you've ever had. No short-cuts, no weird additives or preservatives, just beautiful, artistic, utterly delectable, highly customized treats delivered to your doorstep. What's not to love?
